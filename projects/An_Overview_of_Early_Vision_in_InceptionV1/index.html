An Overview of Early Vision in InceptionV1

Created in December 07, 2024

2024   ·   Summary

https://distill.pub/2020/circuits/early-vision/ first layer of inceptionV1 has three groups of neurons. Gabor filters are like edge detectors. but each of them could only detect edges at a specific angle. Color Contrast filters will be activated if they see one color in one side of their field of view and another color in another side.

Second layer has many groups.

it gets more and more complex as we go deeper.
